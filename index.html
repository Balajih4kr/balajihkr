About Me

Hi, I'm Balaji A., a passionate and self-driven cybersecurity enthusiast with a strong foundation in ethical hacking, web security, and system exploitation. I thrive on discovering real-world vulnerabilities, building security tools, and pushing the limits of systems — ethically — to make them stronger.

My bug bounty journey includes successful findings on platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd. One of my standout reports involved OAuth token leakage via misconfigured redirect URIs — a finding that was not only impactful but also recognized and rewarded.

Whether it's crafting custom exploits, reverse engineering a binary, or automating recon with my own tools, I’m committed to learning, sharing, and becoming a better hacker every day.
